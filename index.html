Key Insights | الرؤى الرئيسية

Critical: Bundle Offers Losing Money

Your bundle offers (internal + external lock, 2 locks + free) are generating losses of -4,430 SAR. The "2 locks + free" bundle alone lost 4,240 SAR. These promotions are eroding your profits significantly.

Critical: Very Low Conversion Rate

Your conversion rate is only 0.13% (90 orders from 67,964 visits). Industry average for e-commerce is 1-3%. This indicates issues with pricing, user experience, or traffic quality.

Warning: High Return Rate

Return rate increased by 39.17% to 16.7% (15 out of 90 orders). This is concerning and may indicate product quality issues or mismatched customer expectations.

Warning: Profit Margin Declining

Despite sales increasing 25.73%, net profit decreased by 27.52%. Your cost of goods sold increased 58.23% which is outpacing revenue growth. Gross margin is only 21.3%.

Strength: Individual Products Profitable

External lock (47.2% margin) and Internal lock (50.1% margin) are healthy. Smart Lock Cards have an excellent 80% profit margin. Focus on these products.

Strength: Strong TikTok Traffic

TikTok drives 51.4% of all traffic (34,961 visits). This is a strong marketing channel. Continue investing here but work on converting this traffic better.

Insight: Mobile-First Audience

89.2% of visitors use mobile devices. Ensure your store is fully optimized for mobile experience, fast loading, and easy checkout on phones.

Insight: Geographic Distribution

Top markets: Jeddah (30.4%), Riyadh (27.6%), Dammam (13%). Consider targeted campaigns and faster delivery options for these cities.

Actionable Recommendations | التوصيات

  • URGENT - Remove or Reprice Bundle Offers: Immediately discontinue the "2 locks + free lock" bundle. It's losing 4,240 SAR. Either increase the price to at least 1,150 SAR or remove it. Review all bundle pricing to ensure at least 20% profit margin.
  • URGENT - Investigate Returns: With 16.7% return rate, contact customers who returned products to understand why. Common issues could be: installation difficulty, product not matching description, or quality concerns. Address root cause immediately.
  • Improve Conversion Rate: With only 0.13% conversion, focus on:
    - Add customer reviews/testimonials prominently
    - Offer installation service as an add-on at checkout
    - Add product videos showing ease of use
    - Implement exit-intent popups with discount codes
    - Ensure fast page load times on mobile
  • Optimize TikTok Campaigns: Since TikTok drives 51% of traffic but conversion is low, ensure your ads target potential buyers (homeowners, not just viewers). Use retargeting to bring back visitors who didn't purchase.
  • Upsell Smart Lock Cards: These have 80% margin. Promote them as add-ons during checkout ("Add spare cards for your family"). Bundle 5 cards with locks at a slight discount while maintaining 60%+ margin.
  • Installation Service Strategy: Installation service shows 0% profit. Either:
    - Price it higher (most customers will pay for convenience)
    - Partner with local technicians for referral fees instead of handling directly
    - Use it as a paid add-on that improves conversion
  • Focus on Jeddah & Riyadh: These cities represent 58% of your traffic. Consider:
    - Same-day delivery options
    - Local installation partnerships
    - City-specific promotions
  • Review Supplier Costs: COGS increased 58% while revenue only grew 26%. Negotiate better rates with suppliers or find alternative sources. Every 5% reduction in COGS adds ~2,300 SAR to profit.
